The Sunshine Boys

Welcome to the official Website of Sunrise Football Club.

Light of the football game.

Established in May 2018, Sunrise Football Club has grown in leaps and bounds to become one of the most recognized teams in the Bulawayo’s upper league of division 3.

Over the years since our formation, we’ve managed to deservingly earn a couple of accolades through hardwork and dedication to our craft.

Our main purpose is to bring out the best in each of our players and to shape them for future success in football. We do this through working with world class coaches and personnel that meet those standards.

We are by no means a perfect club and neither do we claim to be one but we will continue to strive for perfection

Excellency runs in our blood
